ABOUT IRA

IRA is a resource aggreator that is designed to make research of materials easy at the universtity of Zambia. It pulls information from three unza sources namely, UNZA IR, ZAJLIS journal and LIS DOCUMNET ARCHIVE.With the use of this system, the research has been made easy and effective

Service Features

IRA provides the following features

  • A single search box that allows keyword searching as well as the ability to generate complex Boolean queries
  • An advanced search providing detailed options for defining a search
